Getting Started

Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation bar. Fill in your username, email, and password. After submitting, you'll be able to log in and access all features including hosting and joining tournaments.

After logging in, click on your username in the navigation bar. You can update your profile picture, bio, and contact information. Your profile information will be automatically used when joining tournaments.

TournifyX supports multiple popular esports titles including Valorant, CS2, League of Legends, Apex Legends, Rocket League, and Fortnite. When creating a tournament, you can select from our available game categories.

Tournament Management

Navigate to "Host Tournament" from the navigation menu. Fill in the required details:

  • Tournament name and description
  • Game category (Valorant, CS2, etc.)
  • Format (Knockout or League)
  • Match type (Solo, Duo, Squad)
  • Number of participants
  • Registration deadline
  • Entry fee (optional)

Knockout: Single elimination format where losing one match eliminates you from the tournament. Matches progress through stages (Quarter-finals, Semi-finals, Final).

League: Round-robin format where every participant plays against every other participant. Points are awarded based on wins/draws, and rankings determine the winner.

Go to your tournament dashboard, scroll to the fixtures section, and click "Update Result" for any match. Enter the scores for both teams and submit. The system will automatically update the standings and generate next round matches for knockout tournaments.

Joining Tournaments

Browse available tournaments in the "Public Tournaments" section or use a tournament code. Click "Join" on any tournament and your profile information (username, email, avatar) will be automatically registered. For team tournaments, you'll need to provide your team name and IGN (In-Game Name).

Fixtures & Matches

Fixtures are automatically generated when you click "Generate Fixtures" on the tournament dashboard. For knockout tournaments, first round matches are created immediately. Subsequent rounds are generated after all matches in the current round are completed.

Yes! Use the "Hide Fixtures" / "Show Fixtures" toggle button on the tournament dashboard to collapse or expand the fixtures section for better readability.

Knockout tournaments progress through labeled stages: Quarter-finals → Semi-finals → Final. Winners advance to the next round, while losers are eliminated. The system automatically labels and organizes matches by stage.

Payment Issues

After joining a paid tournament, you'll be redirected to the payment page. Follow the payment instructions provided by the tournament host. Once payment is confirmed, your registration will be complete.

If your payment fails, check your payment method and try again. If the issue persists, contact the tournament host directly or reach out to support@tournifyx.com with your transaction details.

Refund policies are set by individual tournament hosts. Contact the tournament organizer before the registration deadline to request a refund. TournifyX facilitates the platform but refund decisions are made by tournament hosts.

Technical Issues

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try accessing from an incognito/private window

This might be due to slow internet connection or browser settings. Ensure JavaScript is enabled, check if your browser blocks autoplay videos, and try refreshing the page. On mobile, some videos may require user interaction to play.

TournifyX is designed to be responsive across all devices. If you're experiencing layout issues on mobile, try rotating your device, updating your mobile browser, or reporting the specific issue to our support team with your device model and browser version.

We appreciate bug reports! Email support@tournifyx.com with:

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the bug
  • Browser and device information
  • Screenshots if applicable
  • What you expected to happen vs what actually happened
